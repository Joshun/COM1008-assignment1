About the company

Jmoey Web Design was founded in 2002 by John Smith with a vision of creating vibrant, modern-looking websites. The company has gone from humble origins to developing websites for major corporations, including IBM and Motorola. It has since acquired a large team of around 200 dedicated staff.

Our company seeks to embrace the new creative technologies emerging on the web, particularly HTML5 and CSS3. Our demo page has more information on this.
